About AlgoMol

Algorithms + Physics + AI, working in concert

We are an early‑stage tech startup focused on molecular structure analysis. Leveraging proprietary algorithms for fast molecular equivalence detection, combined with machine learning and ab initio methods, we deliver accurate, scalable, and user‑friendly analysis for research institutions, pharma, and materials teams.

By tightly integrating AI with physical models, AlgoMol is pushing molecular recognition toward automation and intelligence—bringing a new computational paradigm to the chemical industry.
